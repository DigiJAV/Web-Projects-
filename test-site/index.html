The Course of Empire - Consummation

The walls and columns ring with the noise of crowds, musical instruments, horses and oxen. The sound of fountain water and smells of insense generate a state of calmness to those nearby. The celebration of the struggle of generations past and their consummation in the present begins. The following colors are visible in a fountain:

  • Blue
  • Beige
  • Pearl
  • Marble
  • Obsidian
  • Turquoise
  • Mustard
  • Copper
